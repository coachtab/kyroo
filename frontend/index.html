FAQ

Questions answered.

What exactly is Kyroo?

Kyroo is your AI fitness coach. Pick a program, answer a few guided questions about your body and goals, and Kyroo generates a complete personalized training and nutrition guide as a downloadable PDF.

Is my plan really personalized?

Yes — entirely. Every guide is generated from scratch using your weight, height, age, fitness level, weekly schedule, available equipment, and goals. No two plans are identical.

What's included in the free plan?

The calculators — BMR, 1RM, macros, and body fat estimates — are completely free with no account needed. AI-powered programs require a Basic or Pro plan.

How does the PDF work?

After answering your questions, Kyroo generates your complete guide in about 30 seconds. Read it on screen, download it as a PDF, or print it to bring to the gym.

Can I regenerate if I want changes?

Yes. With Basic and Pro plans you can regenerate your guide at any time. Adjust your answers and get a fresh plan whenever your goals change.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. No contracts, no hidden fees, no commitments.
